How To Invest

After selecting the farm you are interested in..

  1. Invest or Pledge

    2. Once you have decided which farm/farms to invest in, you can either invest or pledge. The minimum investment for this beta run is ₱ 5,000.00 with increments of ₱ 5,000.00.

    • Option A: In order to directly invest, you must have sufficient credits on your virtual wallet. You can do this by adding money to your virtual wallet through bank deposit.

    • Option B: In the case that you haven’t deposited any amount on your virtual wallet yet, you may choose to pledge. All pledges are given 3 working days to deposit the payment. It should be noted that the platform does not limit the amount of pledges. A pledge will only be converted to investment once the deposit has been made and verified by Cropital. The amount deposited should be at least in equivalent value of the amount pledged. Any excess of the deposited amount shall be credited back to the user’s virtual wallet.

  2. Notification & Invoice

    3. You will receive a notification which also serves as your digital invoice as a proof of investment.


  3. Memorandum of Agreement

    4. Once the farm you have selected to invest has successfully completed its funding, you will receive a memorandum of agreement.


  4. Confirmation

    5. Congratulations! You have successfully funded your farm/s. From hereon, you will receive regular updates on the progress of your farms. You need to wait for about 3 to 6 months before the harvest of your crops and to receive back the capital you invested and reap the profit you share.


Charges and Returns

Charges

5% Platform Service Fee charged at the start of investment

Returns

Return is a fixed percentage of the net profit of the farm invested.


This is dependent on the terms agreed upon by the farm community, considering the level of risks involved. This is explicitly shown at the profiles of the farm.
